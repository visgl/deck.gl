Thursday 22nd - Friday 23rd September 2022

CARTO
C/ Gran Vía, 46, 28013 Madrid

Come together with the vis.gl/deck.gl community to discuss the present & future of the leading open source library for geospatial applications.

At the Open Visualization Collaborator Summit, we'll be bringing together an international audience of geospatial minds to discuss how they are using deck.gl to build apps, foster more contribution and envisage the future of the leading open source mapping library. It'll be face-to-face and also streamed online.

GET YOUR TICKET NOW

Where?

We'll be hosting the Open Visualization Collaborator Summit in sunny Spain, at CARTO's offices in the center of the beautiful city of Madrid - on the iconic Gran Vía street.

Who?

Developers who are passionate about building geospatial applications with deck.gl. Come along to build amazing things, meet others facing similar challenges and to enjoy some “tapas and mapas”. This isn't a huge conference, but more of an intimate event - so we can only accept a maximum of 60 people at this event.
