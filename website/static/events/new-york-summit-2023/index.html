Come together with the vis.gl/deck.gl community to discuss the present & future of the leading open source library for geospatial applications.

At the Open Visualization Collaborator Summit, we'll be bringing together an international audience of geospatial minds to discuss how they are using deck.gl to build apps, foster more contribution and envisage the future of the leading open source mapping library.

GET YOUR TICKET NOW

Where?

We'll be hosting the Open Visualization Collaborator Summit in New York City, at Foursquare's offices in the iconic Flatiron district in Manhattan.

Who?

Developers who are passionate about building visualization applications with deck.gl. Come along to share amazing work, meet others facing similar challenges, and to discuss the future of the community. This isn't a huge conference, but more of an intimate event - however a recording will be available later.
